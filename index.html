Joint & Spine Care
Our Joint & Spine Care programme uses authentic Kerala Panchakarma therapies like Kati Basti, Janu Basti, and Greeva Basti to address the root cause of pain — not just the symptoms. Medicated oils penetrate deep into tissues, restoring balance and mobility.
  • Knee Joint Pain — Janu Basti, herbal poultice massage, Pinda Sweda for cartilage repair and pain relief
  • Cervical Pain (Neck Pain) — Greeva Basti, Nasya therapy, and neck muscle relaxation treatments
  • Lower Back Pain — Kati Basti with warm medicated oil, Kizhi therapy, lifestyle correction

Internal Organ Care
Ayurveda has a profound understanding of internal organ health. Our treatments use Panchakarma detox, Rasayana (rejuvenating) herbs, and dietary guidance to support and restore vital organ function naturally.
  • Cardiac Care — Hridaya Basti, Arjuna-based herbal formulations, stress-reduction therapies
  • Liver Disorders — Virechana (therapeutic purgation), Kalyanaka Ghrita, liver-supportive herbs like Kutki and Bhringaraj
  • Kidney Problems — Vasti therapy (enema), Punarnava formulations, dietary and fluid management

Skin & Hair Care
Ayurveda treats skin and hair conditions by addressing internal imbalances (doshas). Our personalised treatments combine herbal formulations, scalp therapies, and dietary advice for long-lasting results — naturally.
  • Hair Fall Treatment — Shiro Abhyanga, Nasya therapy, Bhringaraj & Amla-based oils, internal Rasayana herbs
  • Dandruff Treatment — Medicated hair oils, Shirodhara, antifungal herbal pastes, scalp purification
